# Sales Dataset Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Sales Dashboard developed in Microsoft Excel to analyze sales performance, customer activity, product revenue, and order fulfillment trends. The dashboard transforms raw sales data into actionable business insights through data visualization and summary metrics.

The objective of the analysis was to evaluate overall business performance, identify revenue-generating products, monitor sales trends over time, and assess order status distribution to support data-driven decision-making.

## Business Questions Addressed

* What is the total revenue generated by the company?
* How many orders were processed?
* What quantity of products was sold?
* How many customers made purchases?
* Which products generated the highest revenue?
* How has sales performance changed over time?
* What is the distribution of order statuses?
* Which payment methods are most frequently used?

## Analysis and Insights

## Revenue Performance

The company generated a total revenue of 126,476.2 from 1,200 customer orders. This indicates a healthy level of sales activity across the analyzed period.

## Product Revenue Analysis

Revenue generated across products appears relatively balanced, with products such as Chairs, Laptops, Printers, Tablets, and Monitors contributing significantly to overall sales performance.

## Sales Trend Analysis

The sales trend chart reveals fluctuations in revenue over time, suggesting seasonal variations and changing customer purchasing behavior. Several peaks indicate periods of high demand, while troughs may signal opportunities for targeted marketing initiatives.

## Customer Activity

A total of 1,189 customers participated in purchases, indicating broad customer engagement and a diverse customer base.

## Order Status Analysis

Order statuses were distributed relatively evenly among:

* Cancelled
* Delivered
* Pending
* Returned
* Shipped

This distribution highlights the importance of monitoring fulfillment processes and reducing cancellations and returns to improve customer satisfaction.

## Payment Method Analysis

The dashboard includes an interactive payment method slicer, allowing analysis of sales generated through:

* Cash
* Cash Card
* Debit Card
* Gift Card
* Online Payments

This feature enables stakeholders to understand customer payment preferences and optimize payment options.


## Recommendations

1. Investigate causes of cancelled and returned orders to improve fulfillment efficiency.
2. Focus inventory planning around high-performing products.
3. Leverage peak sales periods through targeted promotions and marketing campaigns.
4. Encourage adoption of preferred payment methods to improve customer experience.
5. Continuously monitor sales trends to identify emerging opportunities and risks.
